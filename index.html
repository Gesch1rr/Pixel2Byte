// how it works

Three steps to a byte array.

01
Drop your image

Drag and drop any image file onto the upload zone, or click to browse. Need to create something from scratch? Switch to the Draw tab and use the built-in pixel art editor.

02
Configure & convert

Set your canvas size, choose a dithering algorithm, pick color mode and tweak the threshold. Hit CONVERT and see a live preview of exactly what will land on your display.

03
Copy & include

Copy the generated C array or download the .h header file. Include it in your Arduino sketch or embedded project — your image is ready to push to any display.
